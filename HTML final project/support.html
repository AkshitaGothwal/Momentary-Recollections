Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I load film into my Polaroid camera?
    Refer to the user manual included with your camera for step-by-step instructions.
  • My prints are coming out too dark. What should I do?
    Adjust the exposure settings on your camera or check if the film is expired.
  • Can I use third-party film with my Polaroid camera?
    We recommend using genuine Polaroid film for the best results.
